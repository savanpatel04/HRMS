HRMS Product (Human Resource Management Service)

This page showcases a product I developed and upgraded during my time as a Software Developer at Communication Crafts. The product is primarily utilized by HR departments for managing employee data and streamlining company administration tasks.

Product Highlights

  • Collaborated with a team of 10 using the Spring framework to enhance a comprehensive Human Resource Management Solution.
  • Streamlined review and approval cycles through workflow automation, boosting productivity and ensuring important tasks are completed on time.
  • Improved HR performance management by tracking and monitoring employee performance with customizable metrics.
  • Enhanced payroll calculation processes by automating payroll management, ensuring accuracy and timely compensation, including bonuses and deductions.
  • Designed and implemented over 30 key functionalities, including employee data management, performance tracking, payroll integration, and document management with automated alerts.
  • Worked closely with HR professionals to optimize the system, ensuring it meets the evolving needs of the department.
  • Generated detailed reports and analytics on employee performance, payroll, and HR activities to support informed decision-making.
  • Conducted thorough testing, bug fixing, and version control using GIT to ensure system stability and seamless code delivery.

Roles & Responsibilities

  • Involved in designing the user interface using JSF.
  • Collaborated in analyzing new requirements to ensure they align with business needs.
